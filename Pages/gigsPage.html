Pong

We needed to make a remake for pong with our own twist to it. So i was planning to make the ball a friends head the ball and in a picture further down you can see that. And i also made the sticks you play with trashcans and at last i edited the background. This was the first ever project we had to make for school.

It was made on a custom engine that school made it was called canvas enigne but we did use c# for it to code. It was also the first ever time i had programmed in my life exept for the entice test we needed to make to get acepted in the school.

But it was verry fun to learn about the details of c# and it was cool to learn a new language. I sadly dont have any more scripts of this project. I only have the screenshot down below.
